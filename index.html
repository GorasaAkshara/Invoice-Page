About Robocoupler

Robocoupler Techno Solutions Pvt Ltd is India’s First Andro Humanoid Robots Manufacturing Company. Which is Located at Visakhapatnam Andhra Pradesh. Which Deals with Comprehensive Robotic Manufacturing and solutions with Real Time Embedded Systems and Mechatronics.

Robocoupler is registered in India at Innovation valley Visakhapatnam, which is a global company provider of comprehensive Robotics solutions and services that deals with the design, construction and application of robots, as well as computer systems for their control, sensory feedback, and information processing.

Vision: Democratize robotics for small and medium enterprises in India.
